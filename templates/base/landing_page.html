FAQ

How do I know if my business needs automation?

If you or your team spend hours on repetitive tasks, manually transfer data between systems, or struggle with inefficiencies, automation can help. During our discovery call, Ill assess your workflow and determine if automation is the right fit for you.

What is the process of working with BotsNBytes?

  • Discovery Call We discuss your business and automation needs.
  • Proposal & Pricing I outline the solution, timeline, and cost.
  • Onboarding & Development I map out, build, and test your automation.
  • Implementation & Training The system goes live, and I train your team.
  • Ongoing Support I provide post-launch assistance if needed.

What happens if something goes wrong after implementation?

We offer post-launch support to ensure everything runs smoothly. If issues arise, We troubleshoot and optimize the system. For businesses that need continuous automation improvements, We also offer ongoing maintenance plans.

How much does automation cost?

Pricing depends on the complexity of your automation needs. Some projects require simple integrations, while others involve advanced workflows. After our initial call, Well provide a customized quote based on the scope of work.

What types of tasks can you automate for my business?

  • Data entry and spreadsheet updates.
  • CRM and lead management workflows.
  • Email marketing and follow-ups.
  • Invoice generation and payment reminders.
  • Inventory tracking and reporting.

If a task is repetitive and rule-based, it can likely be automated.
